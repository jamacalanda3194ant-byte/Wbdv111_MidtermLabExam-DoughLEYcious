Our Story

The goal of our business is to offer freshly baked, affordable, and high-quality cookies that bring joy to customers. It aims to build a strong and trusted brand, attract a large customer base through quality and creativity, and expand its reach both online and in local communities.

Our business solves the common problem of stale or mass-produced pastries by offering pre-order, freshly baked cookies made specifically for each customer. Many customers want high-quality treats that are fresh, reliable, and suitable for gifting or special occasions. By baking per order, we ensure better taste, consistent quality, and a more personalized experience.

Our business has faced challenges with sudden short stocks when unexpected increases in orders occur. Because we operate on a pre-order system to ensure all cookies are freshly baked, high demand at short notice can make it difficult to fulfill every request while maintaining consistent quality and freshness. Other challenges include coordinating orders efficiently, managing production schedules, and ensuring timely delivery, especially during peak seasons or special occasions. Despite these hurdles, we continuously improve our processes to meet customer expectations and provide a reliable, high-quality pastry experience.🍪
